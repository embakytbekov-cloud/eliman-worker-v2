3. Insurance & Risk

ELIMAN does not provide insurance or workplace protection. You assume all risks related to your work, including injuries, damages, accidents, and property loss.

4. Compensation

You are paid per completed job. No guaranteed wage, no benefits.

5. Indemnification

You agree to indemnify and protect ELIMAN from all claims, damages, lawsuits, injuries, or losses arising from your work, tools, actions, or negligence.
